Top 5 Trends in the Paper Industry for 2025

  1. Smart Packaging: Integration of QR codes and NFC tags.
  2. Advanced Recycling: New technologies are making it possible to recycle a wider range of paper products.
  3. Personalization: Custom printing and packaging solutions for e-commerce brands.
  4. Lightweighting: Reducing paper weight without sacrificing strength to lower shipping costs.
  5. Automation: Increased use of automation in paper manufacturing and converting.
