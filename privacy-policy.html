Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Welcome to Abrar Traders. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal and business information when you use our website (https://abrartraders.co.in) and services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to fulfill your B2B requirements and improve our services. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, work email address, phone number, and company details provided via our Quote Request form.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This includes IP addresses, browser types, and page visit durations.
  • Communication Records: Data submitted through our third-party chat widget (Tawk.to) and WhatsApp integrations.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information exclusively for legitimate business purposes:

  • To generate accurate quotes for paper and packaging supplies.
  • To arrange Pan-India shipping and logistics.
  • To communicate with you regarding order statuses or inquiries.
  • To improve website performance and user experience via analytics.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share data with trusted partners necessary for our operations:

  • Form Processing: Formspree (used to route quote requests securely).
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (for website traffic monitoring).
  • Logistics Partners: Freight and shipping companies (only receiving delivery addresses and contact details).

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your theme preferences (Light/Dark mode), and gather anonymous traffic data. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings or our website's consent banner.
