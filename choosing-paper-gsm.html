How to Choose the Right Paper GSM

A comprehensive guide to understanding paper weight (GSM) and selecting the perfect type for your project needs.

What is GSM?

GSM, or Grams per Square Meter, is a measure of paper density. The higher the GSM number, the heavier and thicker the paper. Understanding GSM is crucial for selecting the right paper for your specific application.

Common GSM Weights and Their Uses

  • 80-120 GSM: Ideal for standard office printing, letterheads, and notebooks. Our Bond Paper falls into this category.
  • 120-170 GSM: Perfect for posters, flyers, and brochures. Our Art Paper is a great choice for high-quality prints.
  • 200-300 GSM: Excellent for business cards, invitations, and book covers.
  • 300+ GSM: Used for high-end packaging and durable applications. Our Duplex Board offers great strength in this range.
